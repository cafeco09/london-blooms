How to use it

The quick version, without the waffle.

1 · Start with a type

Cherry, magnolia or wisteria first

That cuts the noise instantly. Bluebell stays hidden unless separate data exists in the feed.

2 · Tap, don’t chase

Map circles open a fixed details panel

No hover chase, no shifting tooltip. The selected spot stays pinned until you choose another one.

3 · Use community confidence

Thumbs up/down tells you whether the estimate feels right

That is more honest than pretending the app knows each tree’s exact stage in real time.

4 · Support

The coffee link is now always visible

There is a support button in the header, the hero, and the mobile bottom bar.
