How to use it

Four steps to finding the perfect spot.

1

Pick a bloom type first

Use the type chips to filter instantly. Cherry, magnolia, wisteria or bluebell — choose one to cut the noise.

2

Tap the map, don't hover

Click any circle to pin it. A details panel appears below the map and stays visible until you choose another spot.

3

Check community confidence

Bloom stage is estimated. Thumbs up/down from other visitors tells you whether the estimate matches reality right now.

4

Cast your own vote

Just been to a spot? Help others by voting whether the bloom looks right. Every vote improves accuracy for everyone.
