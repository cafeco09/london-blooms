When to go

A simple seasonal guide to what usually feels strongest across spring.

Mar
🌸 Cherry — early pockets start showing 🤍 Magnolia — the first showy blooms open
Apr
🌸 Cherry — usually the biggest pink-cloud month 🤍 Magnolia — strongest in early April
Late Apr
💜 Wisteria — starts to climb and trail 🌸 Cherry — fading, with petal carpets underneath
May
💜 Wisteria — strongest later into spring 💙 Bluebell — only where separate data exists
