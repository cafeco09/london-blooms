When to Go

London's bloom calendar — what's flowering and when in spring 2026

Mar
🌸 Cherry — early spots (Aldgate Square, St James's, Ravenscourt) 🤍 Magnolia — opening across Notting Hill, Kew, Regent's Park
Apr
🌸 Cherry — PEAK (Greenwich, Battersea, Kew Walk, Regent's, Victoria Park, Dulwich, St Paul's) 🤍 Magnolia — peak early April then fading by mid-month 💙 Bluebell — early (Highgate Wood, Isabella Plantation, Tower Hamlets Cemetery)
Late Apr
💜 Wisteria — opening (Kynance Mews, Notting Hill streets, Peckham Rye pergola) 💙 Bluebell — PEAK (Highgate Wood, Oxleas Wood, Richmond Park's Bluebell Walk) 🌸 Cherry — fading, pink petal carpet underfoot
May
💜 Wisteria — PEAK (Fuller's Brewery 200yr-old vine, Chelsea streets, Hampstead Pergola, Bedford Gardens) 💙 Bluebell — late spots still carpeted (Oxleas Wood)
